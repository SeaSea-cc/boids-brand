You may use the Boids name, in plain text only, when:

1. **Describing compatibility or integration** — e.g., "Compatible with Boids", "Built for Boids", "Boids-ready", provided your use is truthful and does not suggest endorsement.
2. **Referring to Boids in editorial / journalistic / educational content** — articles, reviews, tutorials, academic work.
3. **Following this skill** — when using `boids-brand` to author Boids-branded content as a Boids customer, partner, or authorized agent.
4. **Linking to Boids properties** — boids.so or any other Boids-owned URL.

In all cases:
- The word "Boids" must be spelled exactly that way (no lowercase variant for the trademark, no compound mark like "Boids-Pro").
- You must not modify, recolor, or reproportion the Boids logo if you display it.
- You must not present your work as an official Boids product or service.

## Applying the Brand System

This skill (`boids-brand`) defines the visual and verbal system for **Boids-owned and Boids-authorized output**. If you are producing such output as an authorized partner, customer, or contractor:

- Follow the rules in `SKILL.md` and `references/`.
- Apply the v1.2 Precision Mono + Ambient Glow palette, Builder Stack typography, and Double-Bezel surface system exactly as specified in `references/brandbook.md`.

If you are **not** authorized to publish Boids-branded output, this skill is provided as a reference for the brand system — please do not apply it to non-Boids work as if it were your own brand.
